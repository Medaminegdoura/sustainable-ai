## üé≠ Key Features

### 1. **Empathy Profiles** (Per Party)

Each party gets a comprehensive psychological profile:

#### Emotional State (8 Options)
- **Collaborative**: Open to working together
- **Defensive**: Protecting interests, guarded
- **Aggressive**: Pushing hard for demands
- **Anxious**: Worried, uncertain
- **Optimistic**: Positive outlook
- **Skeptical**: Doubting, questioning
- **Desperate**: Under pressure, urgent needs
- **Confident**: Sure of position

#### Power Dynamic (5 Options)
- **Equal**: Balanced power
- **Dominant**: Strong position
- **Subordinate**: Weak position
- **Dependent**: Reliant on others
- **Independent**: Self-sufficient

#### Negotiation Style (5 Options)
- **Competing**: Win-lose approach
- **Collaborating**: Win-win seeking
- **Compromising**: Give and take
- **Avoiding**: Withdraw from conflict
- **Accommodating**: Yield to others

#### Cultural Context (7 Options)
- **Western-Direct**: Clear, explicit communication
- **Eastern-Indirect**: Harmony-focused, subtle
- **Middle Eastern**: Relationship-based trust
- **Latin American**: Personal connection first
- **African**: Community-oriented decisions
- **Scandinavian**: Consensus-driven approach
- **Multicultural**: Mixed background

#### Psychological Depth
- **Emotional Triggers**: Things that upset/motivate
- **Core Values**: Fundamental beliefs (fairness, tradition, innovation)
- **Past Experiences**: Historical context shaping perspective
- **Trust Level**: 0-100% trust in other parties
- **Stress Level**: 0-100% current pressure
